Bidding Systems.

Pre defined bidding systems available in ZBridge are the following. You can also define your own systems based on the available Conventions.

SAYC - Standard American Yellow Card.

  • General.
    • Style: American.
    • Open with: 5 Card Majors.
    • Slam Conventions: Blackwood, Gerber, Cue bids.
    • After NT Opening (1NT, 2NT).
      • Stayman: Yes, non forcing.
      • Jacoby Transfers: Yes.
        • 4 Suit Transfers: No.
    • Limit Raises: Yes.
      • Jacoby 2NT: Yes.
    • Splinter Bids: Yes.
    • 4 Suith Forcing: Yes

  • 2 Bids.
    • Weak Two Bids: Yes.
      • 2NT Response: Feature.
      • New Suit Response: Forcing.
    • 2D response on 2C open: Waiting.

  • NT Range.
    • 1NT Open Range: 15-17 points.
    • Open 1NT with weak 5 card major: No.
    • 2NT Open Range: 20-21 point.
    • 3NT Open: 25-27 point balanced.

  • NT Other.
    • 2NT Rebid After 1 Over 1: 17-18 HCP non Forcing.
    • 2NT Rebid After 2 Over 1: 15-18 HCP Game Forcing.
    • After Openers 1NT Rebid: New Minor is Sign Off.
    • After 1NT Openers 3C/3D: Rebid is Game Invitation.
    • After 1NT Openers 3H/3S: Rebid is Slam Invitation.

  • Misc.
    • Reverse Restrictions: Yes.
    • Takeout Doubles: Yes.
    • Negative Doubles: Yes.
    • Michaels Cue Bid: No.
    • Unusual Notrump: No.
    • Drury: No.
    • Jump Overcall Weak: Yes.
      • 2NT Response: Feature.
    • Three Level Preempts: Sound.

  • Leads.
    • Open Leads vs Suit.
      • AK sequence leads A.
      • xxx sequence leads lowest.
    • Open Leads vs NT.
      • AK sequence leads A.
      • xxx sequence leads highest.
    • Length Leads.
      • 4th best: Yes.
      • 3rd/5th Best: No.
      • Low Encouraging: No.

  • Signals.
    • Suit.
      • Partner leads: Attitude: high = positive.
      • Declarer leads: Count: high = even.
      • Discarding: Attitude: high = positive.
    • NT.
      • Partner leads: Attitude: high = positive.
      • Declarer leads: Count: high = even.
      • Discarding: Attitude: high = positive.
