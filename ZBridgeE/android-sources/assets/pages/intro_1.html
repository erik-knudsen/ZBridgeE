Introduction.

Welcome to ZBridge  for Windows.  ZBridge is an easy-to-use program you can use to learn and practice the game of Bridge.


Just what exactly is Bridge?

Bridge is a card game played with four people.  It combines the elements of planning, communication, memory, and logical deduction into a compelling game of skill.


Why should I want to play Bridge?

Well, Bridge is probably the most intricate and complex of all card games, or any games for that matter.  In fact, only chess rivals Bridge for depth of play and subtlety.


How do I play Bridge?

As great as Bridge is, it is not an easy game to learn.  Simply watching a group of people playing the game will not help you learn, but will most likely end up confusing you.  The best way to get into the game is to start from the bottom, learning the rules and trying out practice games.  That's where a computer program like this one can be helpful.

You can find complete instructions on how to play Bridge in the section About Bridge.
