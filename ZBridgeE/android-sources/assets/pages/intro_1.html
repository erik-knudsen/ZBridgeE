Introduction.

Welcome to ZBridge  for Windows.  ZBridge is an easy-to-use program you can use to learn and practice the game of Bridge.


What is Bridge and why should I want to play Bridge?

Bridge is a card game played with four people.  It is an intricate and complex card game and involves many elements of planning, communication, and logical deduction.


How do I play Bridge?

It is not an easy game to learn.  Simply watching a group of people playing the game will not be of much help.  The best way to learn the game is to start from the bottom, learning the rules and trying out practice games.  That's where a computer program like this one can be helpful.

You can find complete instructions on how to play Bridge in the section About Bridge.
